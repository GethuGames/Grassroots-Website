What is?

A Mobile App to connect Volunteers and NGOs in a whole new way.

Local Events

Find Social events from All NGOs happening in your Neighbourhood, filtered by your favorite Cause(s)

Real time Chat

Real time Chat with other Volunteers and Event Coordinator

Venue

Finding the Venue of the event is easy as never before.

Volunteer History

First System to track Volunteering history of Volunteer across NGOs and also make Volunteers accountable

Notification reminders for Volunteers for every event they join
